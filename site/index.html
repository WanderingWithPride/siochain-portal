"Siochain" is the Irish word for peace.
That's what we're here to work toward.

Whatever you're carrying right now — the anxiety that won't quiet down, the grief that doesn't look like grief, the exhaustion of a body that works against you — you deserve a space where none of that has to be explained away.

What we offer

Therapy that meets you where you actually are

Trauma Therapy

Trauma-informed individual therapy for adults. You don't have to retell your whole story from the beginning. We work at your pace, with your safety in mind.

Adults

EMDR Therapy

Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing — an evidence-based approach for trauma and PTSD that helps your brain process what's stuck.

Trauma & PTSD

Play Therapy

For children ages 3–12 who aren't ready for talk therapy — and don't need to be. Play is how children process the world. Sessions help kids work through anxiety, trauma, and big changes.

Children & Families

Chronic Illness Therapy

Chronic Illness & Medical Trauma

A note on chronic illness

Living with a difficult body is its own kind of grief

Most therapy wasn't designed with chronic illness in mind. The "just get some exercise" advice. The appointments you had to cancel. The grief that isn't recognized because you're still alive and functional, mostly, on the outside.

Siochain Hope is a space that understands that kind of exhaustion. You don't have to explain what a flare is, or why you're grieving something that never went away.

  • Chronic illness, pain, and medical trauma
  • Navigating healthcare systems that don't always listen
  • Grief that doesn't look like grief
  • Caregiver fatigue and support
  • Life transitions after a diagnosis

About Maura

Maura Rupp is a Licensed Clinical Social Worker with nearly a decade of clinical experience in outpatient mental health settings in southeastern Pennsylvania. She holds an MSW from West Chester University.

She founded Siochain Hope Counseling because she believes that therapy should feel like a relief — not another thing to manage.
